### Content Structure for SEO
1. **H1:** Primary keyword (Prop Firm Challenge)
2. **H2:** Secondary keywords (Funded Trading Account, AI Signals)
3. **H3:** Long-tail keywords (FTMO Challenge Clearing, Risk Management)
4. **Body:** Natural keyword distribution (2-3% density)
5. **Alt Tags:** Descriptive for all images with target keywords

- **Schema Markup:** FinancialService, SoftwareApplication, FAQPage
- **Core Web Vitals:** Optimized images, lazy loading, critical CSS
- **Internal Linking:** Cross-link related content (signals → risk management → calculator)
- **Mobile Optimization:** Responsive design with touch-friendly interactions
